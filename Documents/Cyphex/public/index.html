ABOUT CYPHEX

In today's digital landscape, data breaches are inevitable. Major corporations, social networks, and even government agencies have fallen victim to cyber attacks, exposing billions of user records.

Cyphex was created to empower individuals with knowledge about their digital security. By checking if your email has been compromised in known data breaches, you can take proactive steps to protect your online identity.

Our mission is to make cybersecurity awareness accessible to everyone, not just tech experts. We believe that understanding your digital footprint is the first step toward better online safety.
